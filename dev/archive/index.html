Welcome

Since 2006, Guide to Game has been Ontario’s one-stop destination for Hunting & Firearms licensing and training services. We offer a variety of courses including: the Canadian Firearms Safety Course (CFSC), the Canadian Restricted Firearms Safety Course (CRFSC), the Ontario Hunter Education Program (OHEP) and the International Bowhunter Education Program (IBEP).

Private coaching sessions and clinics are also offered.

If you're keen to get started in the shooting sports, head over to the training courses section to book today! You’ll need to start with the Canadian Firearms Safety Course, Canadian Restricted Firearms Safety Course, or the Ontario Hunter Education Program.

If you’re already licensed, we also provide property consulting, shooting clinics, or guided hunts.

Please note:

We require pre-payment for all courses.

Deposits are non-refundable, except with proof of extenuating circumstances.

Fees are transferable under said circumstance, or with 7 days notice. This policy ensures class-sizes operate within provincial and federal mandates.

“No-Shows” will forfeit their payment and be subject to a $75.00 re-booking fee in order to register for a make-up course date.

All fees are subject to change without notice.

ALL PRICES INCLUDE HST, ALL COURSE MATERIALS AND EXAMS UNLESS OTHERWISE STATED.

*Any students whom have completed the CFSC within 30 day of taking the CRFSC will receive a government issued discount of $61.00 bringing your total to $124.00.

Guided Hunts

Guide to Game offers premium quality seasonal guided hunts in the Minden Hills and Kawartha Areas.
